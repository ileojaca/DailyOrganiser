# Premium Features Specification - DailyOrganiser

## Executive Summary

This document outlines premium features that transform DailyOrganiser from a task management app into a comprehensive life optimization platform, focusing on work-life balance, family time, rest, and intelligent advisory capabilities.

---

## 1. Family Time Management

### 1.1 Family Calendar Integration
- **Shared Family Calendar**: Sync with family members' calendars
- **Kid Activity Scheduling**: School pickups, sports, activities
- **Family Meal Planning**: Shared meal prep and dinner scheduling
- **Quality Time Blocking**: Dedicated family time blocks

### 1.2 Family Task Management
- **Shared Family Goals**: Household chores, family projects
- **Chore Assignment**: Distribute tasks among family members
- **Family Rewards**: Gamification for completing family tasks
- **Family Dashboard**: Overview of everyone's schedules
